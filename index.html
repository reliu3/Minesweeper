Minesweeper

Choose a Level to Start

Right-click to uncover the tile; Left-click to flag/unflag the tile

The numbers in the squares tell you how many mines neighbor that singular block.

Neighbor blocks are all the blocks that are next to or diagonal to the block

You are given the as many flags as there are mines

Uncover all the unmined tiles to win
